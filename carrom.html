carrom is an indoor tabletop game of skills and strategy,originating in south Asia,where players use a striker to pocket small discs into corner pocket.the game invovles striking a strikers across a smooth,sqaure board,with the goals of pocketing your colored carrom men and the "Queen"disc before the opponent does.success requries precision in flicking the strikers,strategic placement of carrom men,and adherence to scoring and foul rules,making it a popular game in india and around the world.

Objective
The primary objectives is to pocket all your designates carrom men and the red queen disc before your oppoent does.

Equipment
  • carrom board:A square,smooth wooden board with four pockets in the corners.
  • Striker:A larger,heavier disc used to hit the carrom men.
  • Powder:Talcum powder is often used to ensure a smooth surface for the disc to slide.

Rules of Carrom:
  1. Gameplay:players take turns striking the coins with the strikers
  2. pocketing:A player must pocket a coin of their color to continue playing
  3. Queen:The queen must be poketed after pocketing one of your coins
  4. Covering the queen:after pocketing the queen,a player must pocket one of their coin to cover her
  5. Fouls:If a player pockets the striker or commits a foul,their turns ends
