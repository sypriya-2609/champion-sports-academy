Boxing is a sport where two opponents wear padded gloves and exchange punches in a ring,aiming to score points or knock out their opponent within a set number of rounds
Rules of Boxing:
  1. Fight takes place in a ring
  2. Rounds:professional bouts usually last 12 rounds 3 min each 1 min rest amateur bouts are shorter
  3. scoring:judges use the 10 point must system winner of round gets 10 points,opponent gets 9
  4. Fouls: no hitting below the belt,headbutts,hitting after the bell
History of Boxing
  • Ancient origin:Boxing -like contest date back to Mesopotamia,Egypt,and ancient Greece
  • Modern Boxing :The sport was codified in 1867 with the marquess of queensberry Rules,introducing gloves,timed rounds
