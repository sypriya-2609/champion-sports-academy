Football:
Football is also known as Soccer, originated in the mid-19th century.The modern game has evovled overtime,with standardized rules and global competitions
Rules of Football:
  1. Two teams,each with 11 players,take turns playing offence and defence
  2. The game is devided into two 45 min halves
  3. players can use any part of their body except their hands and arms to control and move the ball
  4. points can be scored through goals
